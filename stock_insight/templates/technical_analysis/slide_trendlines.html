📈 Understanding Trendlines

🔍 What are Trendlines?

Trendlines are linear connections drawn between two or more price points on a chart, serving to pinpoint and validate trends. They connect the swing highs and swing lows in a trending market.

📊 What does a Trending Market look like?

An uptrending market is characterized by the formation of higher tops and higher bottoms. A downtrending market is characterized by the formation of lower tops and lower bottoms.

⚙️ Why do we use Trendlines?

1. Trendlines act as a support and resistance.

2. Break of Trendlines can signal a possible trend change.
