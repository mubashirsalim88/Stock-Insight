Charts are powerful tools in technical analysis that help traders visualize price movements and identify trends. Let's dive deeper into how these charts can inform your trading decisions!

üìä Line Charts

Line charts display the closing prices of a security over a specific period, connecting each data point with a continuous line. This simple representation helps traders quickly identify price trends.

üîç Understanding Candlestick Charts

Candlestick charts are one of the most popular tools in technical analysis, providing detailed information about price movements. Each candlestick represents a specific time frame and contains:

  • Open: The price at which the candle opens.
  • High: The highest price reached during the time frame.
  • Low: The lowest price reached during the time frame.
  • Close: The price at which the candle closes.

The body of the candlestick is filled or hollow, indicating whether the closing price was higher or lower than the opening price. Green or white candles typically indicate upward movement, while red or black candles indicate downward movement.
