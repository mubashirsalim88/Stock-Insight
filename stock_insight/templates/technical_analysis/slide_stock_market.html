ğŸ¦ What is the Stock Market?

The stock market is a place where market participants can access any publicly listed company and trade from their point of view, as long as there are other participants who have an opposing point of view. Different opinions are what make a market.

  • Stocks represent ownership: Each share of stock is a small portion of a company's ownership.
  • Supply and demand: Prices of stocks fluctuate based on market forces.
  • Market Participants: Includes retail investors, institutional investors, mutual funds, and more.
