📊 Technical Analysis - Price Action

Price action is the study of price movement over time, helping traders make informed decisions by observing patterns, trends, and historical data. Let's dive into its key aspects!

🌟 Key Concepts in Price Action

  • Price Patterns: Recurring formations that can signal potential movements.
  • Trend Strength: The power and duration of a price trend.
  • Momentum: The speed of price movement, indicating potential reversals or continuations.

🔍 Candlestick Patterns

Candlestick patterns are key tools in price action analysis. Patterns like engulfing, doji, and hammer help traders identify potential price reversals and trends.

📉 Support and Resistance

Support and resistance levels are price zones where a security tends to reverse direction. Traders use these levels to make informed buy and sell decisions.

📈 Trendlines and Channels

Trendlines are used to highlight the direction and strength of a trend, while channels provide insight into price fluctuations between support and resistance levels.
