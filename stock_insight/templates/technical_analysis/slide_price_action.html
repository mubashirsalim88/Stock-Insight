Technical Analysis - Price Action

Price action is the study of the movement of a security's price over time, used by traders to make informed decisions based on patterns, trends, and past movements.

Key Concepts in Price Action

  • Price Patterns
  • Trend Strength
  • Momentum

Candlestick Patterns

Candlestick patterns, such as engulfing, doji, and hammer, are crucial for identifying price reversals and continuations.

Support and Resistance

Support and resistance levels are key areas where the price tends to reverse. Identifying these levels can help traders make informed decisions.

Trendlines and Channels

Using trendlines and channels, traders can predict future price movements by identifying support and resistance levels.
