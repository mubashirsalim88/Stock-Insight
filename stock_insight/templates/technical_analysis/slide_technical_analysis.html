Technical Analysis - Charts

Technical analysis involves studying past market data to predict future price movements. Two important chart types used in technical analysis are line graphs and candlestick charts.

Line Graphs

A line graph connects closing prices over a specified period, providing a simple way to visualize price movements.

Candlestick Charts

Candlestick charts provide a detailed view of price movements within a specific period. Each candlestick shows the open, high, low, and close prices of the stock.
