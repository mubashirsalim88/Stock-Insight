Doji

Indicates indecision in the market, where the opening and closing prices are almost identical.

Bullish Engulfing

A bullish reversal pattern where a larger green candlestick fully engulfs the previous smaller red candlestick.

Bearish Engulfing

A bearish reversal pattern where a larger red candlestick fully engulfs the previous smaller green candlestick.

Hammer

A bullish reversal pattern that forms at the bottom of a downtrend. It has a long lower shadow and a small body.
