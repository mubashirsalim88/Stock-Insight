üîç Understanding Support & Resistance

Support and resistance are key concepts in technical analysis, helping traders identify potential reversal points to make more informed trading decisions.

üõ† What is Support & Resistance?

Support represents a low level that a stock price reaches over time, while resistance represents a high level. Support occurs when a stock price falls to a level that prompts traders to buy, causing the price to stop falling and start rising. Conversely, resistance occurs when a stock price rises to a level that prompts traders to sell, causing the price to stop rising and start dropping.

üìà Key Concepts

  • Support Level: A price level where buying interest is strong enough to prevent the price from falling further.
  • Resistance Level: A price level where selling interest is strong enough to prevent the price from rising further.
