Introduction to Indicators

Indicators are mathematical calculations based on the price, volume, or open interest of a security. They help traders make informed decisions.

Common Indicators:

  • Moving Averages
  • MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
  • RSI (Relative Strength Index)
